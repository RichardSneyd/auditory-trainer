What is NeuroTunetm?

NeuroTunetm is a free dynamic auditory processing trainer! Just select your prefered music file from your device - we recommend calming, stimulating classical music, but it's your choice! Use bone conduction headphones for the best experience.

I developed NeuroTunetm as a tool for my son's home intervention program, and decided to share it for free for those who can't afford expensive premium programs such as Tomatis etc.

NeuroTunetm trains your auditory system by dynamically modulating the audio in ways that stimulate interhemispheric communication, and 'surprise' the auditory system, since these modulations are controled by an algorithm which makes them varied and unpredictable. Frequencies in the human vocal range are emphasised, thus training the brain to focus on 'signal', while filtering noise. Over time this enhances auditory attention and focus, improving auditory descriminiation and processing. Listening becomes more effortless.

Features

  • Filter Frequency Range: Allows you to set a minimum and maximum frequency range in Hertz (Hz). Dynamic high-pass filtering is then applied within these parameters.
  • Gating Frequency Range: Enables you to set the minimum and maximum gating frequency (how often gating events occur) in seconds, adjusting how the sound is processed over time.
  • Volume Control: Gives you the ability to control the maximum volume through a slider, ensuring that volume modulation never goes above the level you specify.
  • Dynamic Filter, Gating & Playback Rate Switch: These options let you enable or disable dynamic filtering and gating, as well as dynamic playback speed, offering further customization to your auditory training. High-pass filtering and gating are on by default.

Usage

  1. Upload an audio file to begin.
  2. Customize your experience using the Dynamic Settings.
  3. Start your auditory training by playing the audio.

Purpose and Benefit

NeuroTunetm is designed to be customisable and versatile in operation and application, So, while I originally designed it for my son's auditory processing disorder intervention program, it can be of use to anyone interested in sharpening their auditory processing skills.

The Science Behind NeuroTune™

The Theoretical Model

NeuroTune™ is based on principles of auditory neuroscience that focus on selective auditory attention and interhemispheric communication within the brain. By dynamically manipulating sound frequencies, particularly those within the human vocal range, it aims to activate specialized neuronal pathways (Zatorre, Robert J., and Isabelle Peretz, eds. "The Cognitive Neuroscience of Music." Oxford University Press, 2003).

Auditory Filters and Frequency Emphasis

Our technology employs specialized filtering algorithms to emphasize certain frequencies while dampening others. This draws from the "cocktail party effect" in auditory scene analysis (Bregman, Albert S. "Auditory scene analysis: The perceptual organization of sound." MIT press, 1994), focusing the brain's auditory attention on 'signal' over 'noise.'

Interhemispheric Communication

The tool uses the dynamic modulation of sound to engage both hemispheres of the brain, promoting interhemispheric communication. This is grounded in research suggesting the involvement of both hemispheres in complex auditory tasks (Rogalsky, Corianne, et al. "Functional anatomy of language and music perception: Temporal and structural factors investigated using functional magnetic resonance imaging." The Journal of Neuroscience 31.10 (2011): 3843-3852).

Randomized Gating and Neuronal Engagement

By incorporating elements of unpredictability via randomized gating, NeuroTune™ stimulates a broader range of auditory neurons. This is consistent with the principle of neural plasticity and the impact of novelty on learning (Buonomano, Dean V., and Michael M. Merzenich. "Cortical plasticity: From synapses to maps." Annual review of neuroscience 21.1 (1998): 149-186).

Customization and Individual Differences

Auditory training programs have traditionally been based around a narrow set of classical music tracks, largely due to the idea of the 'mozart effect'. However, modern research shows that it is often more effective to use music that is to the individuals taste. NeuroTune™ offers more agency to the use via customizable settings, and the ability to choose your own base track from your device. This personalized approach aligns with the individual differences in auditory perception and cognitive engagement (Winkler, István, et al. "Human auditory cortex tracks task-irrelevant sound sources." Journal of Neurophysiology 120.3 (2018): 1269-1280).

The Bottom Line

Incorporating key principles from neuroscience, auditory perception, and educational psychology, NeuroTune™ is engineered to offer a comprehensive, theoretically grounded auditory training program that is freely accessible. Its focus on both the specificity and variability of auditory processing makes it a versatile tool suitable for diverse user needs.
